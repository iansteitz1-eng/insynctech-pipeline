# 6. CONTENT RULES

1. **Every piece must show, not tell.** Use real calls, real numbers, real results.
2. **No AI hype language.** No "revolutionary," "game-changing," "disrupting."
3. **No stock photos.** Real screenshots, recordings, and demos only.
4. **Always include a CTA.** "Comment DEMO," "Link in comments," "Call (727) 334-8156."
5. **Post at optimal times:** LinkedIn 8-10 AM ET Tue-Thu. TikTok 9-11 AM or 7-9 PM ET.
6. **Engage with comments within 1 hour of posting.**
7. **Never automate DM responses.** Personal replies only.
8. **Cross-promote:** LinkedIn post → mention TikTok demo. TikTok → mention LinkedIn for details.
9. **Monthly content audit:** Which posts got engagement? Double down on those formats.
10. **Repurpose:** Every call recording = LinkedIn post + TikTok video + email content.
