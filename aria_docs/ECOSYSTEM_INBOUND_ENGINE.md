## The Journey from Stranger to Customer

```
STAGE 1: DISCOVERY (They Find Us)
├── Google search → Landing page or blog article
├── TikTok/YouTube → Sees a demo video
├── LinkedIn → Sees Ian's post or a connection shares it
├── Podcast → Hears Ian speak
├── Referral → Existing client or partner mentions us
└── Newsletter → Someone forwards an issue
         │
         ▼
STAGE 2: CAPTURE (We Get Their Info)
├── Downloads lead magnet → Email captured
├── Subscribes to newsletter → Email captured
├── Comments "DEMO" on social → Aria DMs with link
├── Calls demo line → Number captured
└── Visits website → Retarget pixel fires (future: paid ads)
         │
         ▼
STAGE 3: NURTURE (We Build Trust)
├── Weekly newsletter → Consistent value delivery
├── Blog/YouTube → They keep finding us in searches
├── Social → They see Ian's content regularly
└── Automated email sequence → 5-email value sequence after lead magnet download
         │
         ▼
STAGE 4: CONVERT (They Buy)
├── Books Calendly demo → Ian closes
├── Clicks Stripe link from email → Self-service purchase
├── Calls demo line → Aria handles, forwards to Ian if hot
└── Partner referral → Pre-qualified, warm intro
         │
         ▼
STAGE 5: EXPAND (They Grow)
├── Upsell to higher tier
├── Expand to more locations
├── Refer others (referral program)
└── Become case study / testimonial
```

## Lead Magnet Email Sequence (5 Emails After Download)

```
Email 1 (Immediate): Deliver the lead magnet + "here's what you'll find inside"
Email 2 (Day 2): One key insight from the report + "here's what businesses are doing about it"
Email 3 (Day 4): Case study or demo recording + "hear it for yourself"
Email 4 (Day 7): ROI calculator specific to their vertical + "what's this worth for you?"
Email 5 (Day 10): Direct offer + "free pilot, no risk" + Calendly link
```

After Email 5: Add to weekly newsletter. Continue nurturing until they convert or unsubscribe.

**Aria manages this entire funnel autonomously.** Lead magnet download triggers
the 5-email sequence via Resend. Each email = CertusOrdo transaction.
