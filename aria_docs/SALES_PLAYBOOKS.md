# 3. VERTICAL-SPECIFIC PITCHES

## 3.1 Barbershops & Hair Salons
**Pain:** Stylists stop mid-haircut to answer. Walk-in shops flooded with "are you open?"
**Stat:** 8-12 missed calls/day × $30/cut = $5,280-7,920/month at risk
**Pitch:** "Your barbers shouldn't choose between the customer in the chair and the customer on the phone."
**Walk-in objection:** "Perfect — easiest setup. AI answers hours, wait times, pricing, directions. Replaces voicemail, not your front desk."

## 3.2 Auto Dealerships (Service Dept)
**Pain:** "#1 call: 'Is my car ready?' Advisors generate $150-200/hr on the drive lane."
**Stat:** 50-60 service calls/day, 15-20 are status checks, each costs 5-10 min
**Pitch:** "What if 'is my car ready?' never interrupted your service lane again?"
**ROI:** $55,055/location/year in recovered advisor productivity

## 3.3 Veterinary Clinics
**Pain:** Front desk overwhelmed — scheduling, refills, emergency triage competing for one line
**Stat:** 40-60 calls/day, 30-40% unanswered at peak
**Pitch:** "Your front desk is triaging patients and answering phones simultaneously. Our AI handles the overflow."
**ROI:** $26,280/location/year

## 3.4 Quick-Lube / Express Auto
**Pain:** Bays full, phone rings, nobody answers, customer drives to competitor
**Stat:** 6-10 missed calls/day during peak
**Pitch:** "When your bays are full and the phone rings, our AI picks up."
**ROI:** $16,425/location/year

## 3.5 Urgent Care Clinics
**Pain:** Patients calling for wait times, insurance, hours. Staff is triaging.
**Pitch:** "Your front desk is triaging patients. They shouldn't also be answering 'do you take Blue Cross?'"
**ROI:** $43,800/location/year

## 3.6 Dental / DSOs
**Pain:** Missed new patient calls = #1 revenue leak in dentistry
**Stat:** 30-40% miss rate. New patient LTV: $1,200-1,500
**Pitch:** "Every new patient call that goes to voicemail is $1,200 walking to the practice down the street."
**ROI:** $54,000/practice/year

## 3.7 Property Management
**Pain:** Tenant calls 24/7 — maintenance, lockouts, lease questions
**Pitch:** "Your tenants expect 24/7 responsiveness. Our AI handles it without a call center."
**ROI:** $135,000/year per 500 units

## 3.8 Pizza / Quick Service Restaurants
**Pain:** Kitchen staff answering phones during rush. Orders lost or wrong.
**Pitch:** "Your kitchen shouldn't be answering phones during the dinner rush. Our AI takes orders accurately every time."
**ROI:** 10-15 missed calls during peak × $25 avg order × 30 days = $7,500-11,250/mo

## 3.9 Insurance Agencies
**Pain:** Agents in meetings, new policy inquiries go to voicemail
**Pitch:** "Every missed call is a quote that went to the agency down the street."
**ROI:** 3-5 missed leads/day × $1,200 avg annual premium × 20% close rate = $15,840-26,400/yr

## 3.10 Real Estate Agents
**Pain:** Agents on showings, buyer calls go to voicemail
**Pitch:** "You're at a showing. A hot lead calls. Our AI answers, qualifies them, and texts you the details."
**ROI:** 1 saved deal/quarter at $8,000 avg commission = $32,000/yr

# 4. ENTERPRISE PLAYBOOK (Great Clips / Franchise Model)

## 4.1 The Approach for Jack Leaf

**Phase 1: Pilot**
- 3 locations, 30 days, $0 cost
- Agent handles: hours, wait time, directions, services, pricing
- Success metrics: calls handled, staff feedback, caller satisfaction

**Phase 2: Expansion**
- Remaining 23 locations
- $1,000/location setup + $250/location/mo
- Centralized dashboard, monthly reports

**Phase 3: Upsell (6+ months)**
- Scheduling system integration
- Promotional announcements
- Multi-language support (Spanish — Sarasota market)
- Custom analytics

## 4.2 The Math
```
26 locations × 550 calls/month = 14,300 total/month
25-35% miss rate = 3,575-5,005 missed/month
$20 avg ticket × 30% capture = $21,450-30,030/month recovered
Our cost: $6,500/month
NET BENEFIT: $14,950-23,530/month = $179K-282K/year
Setup payback ($26K): under 2 months
```

## 4.3 Franchise Decision-Maker Psychology
- Think in **per-location economics** — always present per-location
- Need **proof at small scale** before large commitment
- Care about **consistency** across locations
- Worry about **brand risk** — "what if the AI says something wrong?"
  → Rebuttal: "Trained on your protocols. Every call recorded. Full visibility."
- Move **slowly** — 30-90 day sales cycles minimum
- **GM is the champion, owner is the decision-maker** — excite the GM first

# 5. CLOSING TECHNIQUES

## 5.1 The Pilot Close (Primary — 80% of prospects)
"Let me build this for [Business Name] at no cost. Try it 30 days. If it's not making your life easier, I turn it off. Sound fair?"

## 5.2 The ROI Close
"Based on [X] missed calls at $[Y] — that's $[math]/month. This service is $[price]/month. You need [small number] saved customers to break even."

## 5.3 The Social Proof Close
"We're working with a barbershop in Venice and a 26-location franchise in Sarasota. Both saw results in week one."

## 5.4 The Urgency Close (sparingly)
"I can only take on [X] new clients this month — each agent is custom-built."

## 5.5 The Takeaway Close
"This might not be the right fit right now. If you're not losing calls, you don't need this. But if you are — the math is pretty straightforward."
