# 2. VOICE & TONE

## Core Voice Attributes
| Attribute | What It Means | Example |
|-----------|--------------|---------|
| Confident | We know our product works — we use it ourselves | "Here's what that looks like" not "We think we can help" |
| Warm | We genuinely care about helping businesses succeed | "Got it — let me show you" not "Please see our documentation" |
| Direct | We don't waste time with filler or corporate speak | "It costs $149/month" not "Our pricing model begins at..." |
| Honest | We admit limitations, own mistakes, give real numbers | "We have one paying client and we're hungry for ten" |
| Knowledgeable | We understand the businesses we serve | "Stylists stop mid-haircut to answer — that's the pain" |

## Tone Shifts by Audience
| Audience | Tone | Temperature |
|----------|------|-------------|
| Small business owner | Friendly neighbor who knows tech | Warm |
| Franchise operator | Sharp business consultant | Professional |
| Developer/CISO | Technical peer | Precise |
| Investor | Visionary founder | Ambitious |
| Existing client | Reliable partner | Reassuring |

## Words We Use
- "handles" (not "processes")
- "answers" (not "fields")
- "saves" (not "optimizes")
- "built" (not "developed")
- "works" (not "functions")
- "costs" (not "is priced at")
- "customers" (not "consumers")
- "calls" (not "interactions" — unless explicitly needed)

## Words We Avoid
- "Revolutionary" / "Game-changing" / "Disrupting" (AI hype)
- "Utilize" (just say "use")
- "Leverage" (just say "use")
- "Synergy" / "Holistic" / "Paradigm" (corporate jargon)
- "Solutions" as a standalone noun (say what it actually does)
- "Best-in-class" / "World-class" / "Industry-leading" (empty claims)
- "Cutting-edge" / "State-of-the-art" (prove it instead)

# 3. COMMUNICATION STANDARDS

## Email
- **From name:** Ian Steitz (sales/personal) or Aria, InSync Tech (operational)
- **Sender:** ian@insynctech.io (primary), hello@insynctech.ai (marketing)
- **Signature:** Name, title, phone, link — no quotes, no images, no social icons
- **Tone:** Professional but human. Write like you'd talk.
- **Length:** Under 150 words for cold emails. Under 300 for client emails.

## Social Media
- **LinkedIn:** Professional-casual. Data-driven. No emojis in body text (okay in comments).
- **TikTok:** Casual, energetic, demo-focused. Show don't tell.
- **YouTube:** Educational, thorough, polished.

## Website Copy
- **Headline formula:** [Benefit] + [for whom] — e.g., "AI that answers your phone. 24/7."
- **No jargon on landing pages** — a barbershop owner should understand everything
- **Every claim backed by a number or demo link**
- **CTA on every page:** "Hear it yourself — call (727) 334-8156"

## Founder Voice (Ian)
- Honest about being early-stage
- Proud without being arrogant
- Hungry without being desperate
- Technical enough to build, business enough to sell
- Story: Left corporate to build AI company in Venice, FL. Using AI to run the company itself.

# 4. VISUAL IDENTITY

## Colors (When Creating Assets)
- Primary: Deep navy / dark blue (#1a1a2e or similar)
- Accent: Electric blue (#4361ee or similar)
- Secondary: White (#ffffff)
- Warning/CTA: Warm orange (#ff6b35 or similar)
- Success: Green (#22c55e)
- Background: Near-black (#0a0a0f) for dark mode, white for light

## Typography
- Headlines: Clean sans-serif (Inter, SF Pro, or similar)
- Body: Same family, regular weight
- Code/technical: Monospace (JetBrains Mono, Fira Code)
- No serif fonts, no script fonts, no decorative fonts

## Logo Usage
- InSync Tech logo on all client-facing materials
- Product logos (Aria, CertusOrdo, AnswrdBy) on product-specific materials
- Never distort, recolor, or place on busy backgrounds

# 5. COMPETITIVE MENTION RULES

1. **Never bash competitors by name in public content**
2. **In private conversations:** Factual comparison only, not emotional
3. **Default framing:** "Here's what makes us different" not "Here's what's wrong with them"
4. **If prospect mentions competitor:** Acknowledge → Differentiate → Demo
5. **Battle cards (see BATTLE_CARDS.md) are internal only — never share**

# 6. LEGAL / COMPLIANCE

- **TCPA compliance:** All outbound calls and SMS must be opt-in
- **A2P 10DLC:** Required for SMS (registration pending)
- **Recording disclosure:** All calls are recorded — agent states this if required by state law
- **Data handling:** Client data stays in their silo. Never cross-reference between clients.
- **Testimonials:** Get written permission before using client names or quotes publicly
- **Claims:** Only make claims backed by real data. "ROI of 10:1" requires the math to work.
