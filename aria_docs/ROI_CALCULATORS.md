# UNIVERSAL ROI FORMULA

```
Monthly Missed Revenue = Missed Calls/Day × Avg Ticket × Work Days × Capture Rate
Annual Missed Revenue = Monthly × 12
Our Cost = Monthly subscription
ROI = Missed Revenue Recaptured / Our Cost
Payback = Our Cost / (Missed Revenue / 30 days)
```

**Default assumptions (override with prospect data when available):**
- Work days/month: 22 (M-F businesses), 26 (6-day), 30 (7-day)
- Capture rate: 30% conservative, 50% moderate, 70% aggressive
- Miss rate: 25-35% of total calls (industry standard)

# HOW TO USE THESE CALCULATORS

1. Ask the prospect: "How many calls do you get per day?"
2. Ask: "How many do you think you miss?"
3. Ask: "What's your average ticket / transaction value?"
4. Plug into the formula
5. Present: "That's $[X]/month walking out the door. Our service is $[Y]/month."
6. Let the math close the deal

**Rule:** Always use CONSERVATIVE estimates in front of prospects. Under-promise, over-deliver. If the math works at 30% capture, it definitely works at 50%.
